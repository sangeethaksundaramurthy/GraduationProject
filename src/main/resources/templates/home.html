Be aHand Vision and Mission

Be aHand is the Stockholm City's social food service that do good for both people and the environment. With us, you contribute to reduce food waste and the needy get the food for free.

The food supply consists exclusively of food that is donated from the restaurants or private parties. The mission of Be aHand is to serve food not only to who register to the website but also to other vulnerable groups like people experiencing homelessness.
